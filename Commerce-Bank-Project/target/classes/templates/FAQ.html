Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this Finance Tracker?

Finance Tracker helps users organize their personal finances by tracking income, expenses, budgeting, and providing insights into spending patterns. It offers tools for setting financial goals for your future, managing payments, and visualizing financial progress.

How can I create an account?

To create an account, click on the “Register” button on the homepage, enter your details (name, username, email, and password), and follow the on-screen instructions. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, all data is secure, security is a top priority. We use end-to-end encryption to ensure that all sensitive data is stored securely. Additionally, all accounts are protected with secure passwords and optional two-factor authentication.
